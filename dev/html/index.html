Stretch-n-Grow, the world’s largest network of certified youth fitness trainers, originated in Galveston, Texas in 1992. We have expanded to over 1,000 cities across The United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, Guatemala, England, Ireland, Portugal, Australia, New Zealand, Manila, Indonesia, Hong Kong, and Africa! After an intense study and the discovery of alarming statistics concerning the health status of America’s young children, the founders of Stretch-n-Grow set out on their mission to change the course for children everywhere. Millions of children have participated in Stretch-n-Grow!


Stretch-n-Grow of the Rockies is an enrichment program that focuses on creating healthy kids. SNG of the Rockies believes healthy kids create healthy adults. Ask your school if they offer Stretch-n-Grow of the Rockies as a way to help create healthy kids. SNG of the Rockies teaches your kids about kids nutrition (healthy snacks), kids workouts, hygiene, water, and rest.


We serve Broomfield, Westminster, Thornton, Northglenn, and Arvada.
