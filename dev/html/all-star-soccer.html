All Star Soccer

ENROLL IN THIS CLASS

Our All-Star Soccer Classes are non-stop action and fun while learning the fundamentals of the most popular sport in the world!


Our All-Stars learn teamwork (in a non-competitive environment), eye-foot coordination, speed, agility, and balancing skills. Our dedicated coaches teach teamwork and character development.


  • Offered:Seasonal
  • Ages:3-12 Years
  • Class time:30-60 min
  • Qualified Youth Fitness Coach

Highlights of All Star Soccer

  • Non-stop action and fun using soccer equipment in a non-competitive environment Participation for every child
  • Cardiovascular endurance, speed, strength and flexibility training in every class
  • Activities designed to improve motor skills, agility and coordination
  • Discussions on exercise, safety, sportsmanship and soccer
  • Basics of soccer including dribbling, kicking, jumping and tossing or throwing
