About Stretch-n-Grow

Stretch -n- Grow is the world’s largest network of certified youth fitness instructors providing fun & engaging enrichment programs that get kids moving at their schools.


We specialize in high quality, FUN exercise experiences that get kids moving, including: Fitness, Dance, Gymnastics, Sports, Music and Yoga.

What started as a dream to get kids more active and physically fit expanded into building Stretch- N-Grow. Kendra Urban has been a Stretch-n-Grow owner since 2006, having served hundreds of thousands of families over the years.


We serve Southlake, Colleyville, North Richland Hills, Keller, Downtown, Central & NW Fort Worth, Weatherford, Southern DFW, & Ellis County.
