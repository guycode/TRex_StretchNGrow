All Star Gymnastics

ENROLL IN THIS CLASS

We bring the gym to your school! All-Star Gymnastics incorporates independent movement and basic social skills while continuing to develop confidence in gross motor skills and beginning tumbling moves and skills. We believe gymnastics teaches body control and awareness, strength, flexibility, balance, coordination and endurance, all while building self-esteem. These skills can be transferred to future sports and a life-long love of movement.


Most of our schools include All-Star Gymnastics as a fun addition to their weekly curriculum. This class is also offered as an Elective Enrichment Program at select locations.


All-Star Gymnastics 5 Points Of Success

  • Encourage structured physical activity through tumbling
  • Elevate balance and flexibility skills with self-control
  • Escalate development of forward, backward, motor, and cooperative skills
  • Engage in challenging core strength, agility, and endurance activities
  • Equip stars for future gymnastics progression
