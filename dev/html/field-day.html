ENROLL IN THIS CLASS

Bring the EXERCITEMENT of SNG outdoors with this Interactive Festival of FUN! Stretch-n-Grow brings unique activities to your facility with our Field Day program designed for both preschool and school-aged children!


End the year with a BANG & get an amazing BANG for your BUCK! We provide Run-Through Banners for each class to decorate too!


Whether at your facility or the destination location of your choice, your children will enjoy FUN, team-building activities like Relay Races, Obstacles Courses, Potato Sack Races, Tug of War & much more.


Our STELLAR STAFF provides motivation at every station! We encourage teamwork and good sportsmanship.


Field Days are available for as few as 35 and as many as 500 children and typically last about 2 hours. (Age appropriate for children 3 - 12)


PLUS EVERY child receives an award!


We also offer a "Wild-n-Wacky" Waterday!
